Industry Standards Compliant

Nimanic works with all industry standards, including Multi Drop Bus (MDB) from the National Automated Machines Association (NAMA), Executive (also known as Protocol-A) and DTS/EPS from the European Vending Association (EVA).

Virtually any machine built after 1992 complies with at least one of these standards, so you can safely install Nimanic on your machine.

Use one of our partners’ telemetry/cashless device.

Nimanic works on the software level, not on the hardware level. This way we can focus on offering you and your customers the best mobile interaction features while letting telemetry and cashless providers focus on what they do best. Virtually any existing MDB/EXEC/EVA compatible telemetry/cashless device can be integrated with Nimanic, offering your customers the best from both.

Contact us for information about devices and machines that are already pre-configured to work with Nimanic. If you choose any of our partners’ devices you will be Mobile Interactions enabled in just minutes!
